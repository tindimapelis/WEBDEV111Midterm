PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated May 07, 2026

OVERVIEW
Creative Minds Consortium (CMC) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we may collect, use, store, and protect information that you share through the website and related services.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
  • contact us through forms, email, or inquiries;
  • register for an account, event, or activity;
  • submit creative works, announcements, or other content; and
  • interact with services, features, or organization updates on the site.
This information may include your name, email address, message content, account details, and any other information you choose to submit.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
We may use the information we collect to:
  • respond to inquiries and provide support;
  • manage registrations, submissions, and member-related activity;
  • coordinate events, announcements, and organization communication;
  • maintain the security and proper function of the website; and
  • improve the site experience and future organization services.

3. HOW WE SHARE INFORMATION
We do not intentionally sell personal information. Information may only be shared when necessary for organization operations, legal compliance, technical support, or authorized internal coordination related to the website and CMC activities.

4. DATA RETENTION
We keep submitted information only for as long as needed to support organization communication, records, account activity, event coordination, and legitimate site operations, unless a longer period is required by law or policy.

5. DATA SECURITY
We take reasonable steps to help protect the information shared through this site. However, no website or online system can guarantee absolute security, and you use the Services at your own discretion.

6. THIRD-PARTY LINKS
This website may include links to third-party platforms such as Facebook, email services, or other external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party services once you leave our site.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
If you are a minor, you should use the Services with the knowledge and permission of a parent, guardian, or authorized school representative when appropriate.

8. YOUR RIGHTS AND REQUESTS
You may contact us to ask about, correct, update, or request deletion of information you have submitted through this website, subject to any legal or administrative requirements that may apply.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect operational, organizational, or legal changes. Continued use of the website after updates are posted means you accept the revised version.
