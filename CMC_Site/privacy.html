Privacy Notice

Creative Minds Consortium respects your privacy. If you contact us, sign up, or join one of our activities, we may collect the basic details you choose to share, such as your name, email address, and message content.

That information may be used to respond to inquiries, coordinate organization activities, improve member support, and share updates related to the community when appropriate.

We do not intentionally sell personal information. Shared details are handled for site communication, internal records, and organization support purposes only.

If you would like to ask about, update, or request removal of submitted information, you may contact us at cmc2024.official@gmail.com.
