Marahuyo: Mahika ng Pelikula

The official BSEMC film festival celebrating the magic of film and digital storytelling, inspired by the word marahuyo—to be enchanted. From bold ideas to moving visuals, these stories reflect the passion and vision of emerging filmmakers ready to leave their mark on the screen.
